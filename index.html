What exactly does a Data Business Analyst do?

A Data Business Analyst bridges the gap between raw data and business decisions. I help you analyze business data to identify trends, find hidden opportunities, set up KPIs, and provide actionable insights for better decision-making.

How much does data analytics services cost?

My services start from PKR 25,000/month, but the exact cost depends on your business size, data complexity, and scope of work. Most clients see 3-5x ROI on their investment within the first 3 months.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients see initial insights within 1-2 weeks, quick wins in 3-4 weeks, and significant improvements within 60-90 days as we implement data-driven optimizations.

What if I don't have any data tracking setup?

No problem! I help businesses at every stage - from setting up basic tracking systems to organizing existing data. Many successful projects started with clients who had no data tracking at all.
