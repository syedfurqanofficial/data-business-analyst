Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does a Data Business Analyst do?

A Data Business Analyst bridges the gap between raw data and business decisions. I help you:

  • Analyze your business data to identify trends and patterns
  • Find hidden opportunities for growth and cost-saving
  • Set up key performance indicators (KPIs) to track progress
  • Provide actionable insights for better decision-making
  • Create dashboards to monitor business health in real-time

  • Size of your business - Small businesses vs enterprises
  • Complexity of your data - Simple sales data vs multi-source data
  • Scope of work - Basic reporting vs advanced analytics
  • Time commitment - Monthly vs project-based

What if I don't have any data tracking setup?

No problem at all! I help businesses at every stage:

  • Starting from zero: I'll help set up basic tracking systems
  • Existing data: I'll organize and analyze what you have
  • Manual records: Even Excel sheets can be turned into insights
  • Automated systems: Set up ongoing tracking and reporting

Many of my most successful projects started with clients who had no data tracking at all!
