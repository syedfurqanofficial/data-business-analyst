Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with as a data analyst?

I work with businesses of all sizes across various industries including retail, finance, healthcare, manufacturing, technology, and services. Whether you're a startup looking to establish data-driven processes or an established company wanting to optimize your analytics, I can tailor solutions to your specific needs. My experience ranges from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity:

  • Basic dashboard/report: 2-4 weeks
  • Comprehensive data analysis: 4-8 weeks
  • Predictive analytics project: 8-12 weeks
  • Full BI implementation: 3-6 months

I provide detailed timelines after initial consultation based on project scope.

What data do I need to provide for analysis?

This depends on your business goals. Common data sources include:

  • Sales transaction data
  • Customer databases/CRM data
  • Financial statements and records
  • Website/social media analytics
  • Operational/process data
  • Marketing campaign results

If you're unsure what data you have or need, I can help you identify and collect relevant data during the initial assessment.

What tools and technologies do you use?

I work with a variety of tools based on project requirements:

  • Data Analysis: Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn), R, SQL
  • Visualization: Tableau, Power BI, Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Databases: MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Google BigQuery
  • Other Tools: Excel/Google Sheets, Jupyter Notebooks, Git

I select tools based on your existing infrastructure, team skills, and project requirements.

How do you ensure data privacy and security?

Data security is my top priority. I implement several measures:

  • Secure data transfer (encrypted connections)
  • Data anonymization when possible
  • Secure storage on encrypted drives
  • Strict access controls
  • NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) for every project
  • Compliance with relevant data protection regulations

I'm happy to sign additional confidentiality agreements specific to your industry requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer flexible pricing based on project scope:

  • Hourly Rate: PKR 3,500/hour for consultation and small tasks
  • Project-based: Fixed price for defined scope projects
  • Retainer: Monthly packages for ongoing analytics support
  • Training: Customized training programs for your team

I provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements during a free initial consultation.
